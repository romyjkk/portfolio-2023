THE XENOMORPH TIMELINE

Concepting, Design & Front-end Development

HTML/CSS/JS

SPOILERS UP AHEAD!

INTRODUCTION

The timeline had to be unique to the character, with a fitting design and semantic HTML, with a bunch of interactions and even an easter egg. I chose to make the timeline in the shape of vents, since this is the aliens primary travel method through ships. If you want to check it out, I advise you to turn your sound down a bit!

Check it out!

* Viewer descretion is advised and it works best on laptop screens

PROCESS

To start with this project, I first needed a concept. Once I knew what character I was going to work with, I just had to figure out how I was going to design the timeline. I had a few different ideas:

My preference went to the second one, since I thought this would be the best looking. When I asked my teacher for some feedback however, he told me this idea isn’t really unique to the Alien franchise. There’s many sci-fi movies with cool, futuristic space ships. He recommended me to go for the first one, because in which other movie can you think of the enemy crawling through vents?

For the content, I chose for every single part of the Xenomorph’s life and the different mutations exist. I decided to go from movie 1-6, excluding Alien vs Predator and any of the games.

Some of the design choices are based on the game Alien: Isolation, like when you click on a vent. The popup design is based on the terminals you see throughout the game. The easter egg I added is another example, this is the motion-tracker you carry with you to check if any enemies are nearby. The beeping noise and the dot let you know it’s getting closer or further away.

RESULTS

For the time we were given, I’m proud of how far I came with this project. There’s definitely things I still want to improve someday, like the way I added the content. Currently this is a bunch of JavaScript eventListeners that add content on click, but my teacher adviced me to put all the data in a JSON file and use a JavaScript loop to retrieve the data and put it in the right spot.
